Penut Butter cookie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups White Sugar
  • 3/4 cup Butter
  • 3/4 cup Milk
  • 1 & 1/2 cups Peanutbutter
  • 1/2 teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • 4 & 1/2 cups Quick-Cooking Oats

Baking Instructions:

  1. Combine sugar, butter, and milk in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Bring to a rapid boil and boil for 1 minute.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in peanut butter and vanilla.
  4. Mix in oats and stirr until the mixutre begins to cool.
  5. Make small balls with batter and place onto wax paper to cool.
  6. Enjoy!
