Macaron Cookie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 3 Egg Whites
  • 1/4 cup White
  • 1 & 2/3 cups Confectioners' Sugar
  • 1 cup finely ground almonds

Baking Instructions:

  1. Line a baking sheet with a parchment paper.
  2. In a stand mixer with a fited whisk attachment, beat egg whites until foamy.
  3. Add white to foamy egg whites and beat until egg whites are glossy, fluffy, and hold soft peaks.
  4. Spoon a small amount of batter into a plastic bag with a small corner cut off and pipe a test disk of batter, about 1 & 1/2 inch in diameter, onto the prepared baking sheet. If the disk of batter holds a peak insted of flattening immediately, gently fold batter a few more times and retest.
  5. When the batter is mixed enough to flatten immediately into an even disk, spoon into a pastry bag fitted with a plain round tip. Pipe batter onto the baking sheet in rounds, leaving space between the disks. Let piped cookies stand out at room temperature until they form a hard skin on top, about 1 hour.
  6. Preheat the oven to 285 degrees F.
  7. Bake cookies in preheated oven until set but not browned, about 10 mins.
  8. Let cookies cool completely before filling, about 30 mins.
  9. Enjoy!
