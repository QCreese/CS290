Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe!


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Butter or Margarine, softened
  • 3/4 cup Sugar
  • 3/4 cup Brown Sugar, packed
  • 2 Eggs, large
  • 2 teaspoons Vanilla Extract
  • 2 & 1/4 cups Flour, unsifted
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 2 cups Bittersweet Chocolate Chips

Baking Instructions:

  1. Heat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Stir flour with baking soda and salt; set aside for later.
  3. In a large bowl, mix together the butter, sugar, and brown sugar at medium speeds until the mixture is creamy and light in color.
  4. Add eggs and vanilla into the large mixing bowl and mix at low speeds.
  5. Gradually add dry mixture into the large mixing bowl and stir until mixture sticks together
  6. Stir in chocolate chips to mixture.
  7. Add small handrolled cookies onto a greased cookie sheet.
  8. Bake for 9 to 12 minutes or until cookies are golden brown.
  9. Enjoy!
